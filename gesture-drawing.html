Gesture Drawing

This prototype was created to empower children with limited mobility to be able to participate in creative expressions such as drawing. We believe that their physical disabilities should not be a barrier to art-making. Launch this project on a computer or laptop with a webcam and follow the instructions to start calibrating your hand poses! You can begin with an open palm and a closed fist, but if you are unable to do that, any other 2 distinct poses will work too. We recommend using the Google Chrome browser for this. Creative engineering by Koto Club (Clara Koh and Kaori Toh)
